Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer gemstone certification?

Yes, all our gemstones come with proper certification from recognized laboratories like GIA, SSEF, or Gübelin.

Can I schedule a private consultation?

Absolutely! We offer private consultations by appointment. Call us to schedule a one-on-one session with our experts.

Do you accept custom orders?

Yes, we specialize in sourcing specific gemstones based on your requirements. Contact us with your specifications.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, wire transfers, and certified checks for high-value purchases.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide with full insurance and tracking. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unmodified gemstones with original certification and packaging.
