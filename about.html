About JpGems

Your Premier Destination for Exceptional Gemstones

Our Story

Founded in 1995, JpGems has been at the forefront of the gemstone industry for over 25 years. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most trusted names in precious and semi-precious gemstones worldwide.

Our passion for exceptional quality and customer satisfaction has driven us to source the finest gemstones from around the globe. From the diamond mines of Africa to the sapphire deposits of Kashmir, we travel the world to bring you stones of unparalleled beauty and authenticity.

Our Mission

To provide our customers with the highest quality gemstones at competitive prices, backed by expert knowledge and exceptional service. We believe that every gemstone tells a story, and we're here to help you find the perfect stone to tell yours.

Our Values

Authenticity

Every gemstone in our collection is certified authentic and comes with detailed documentation of its origin and characteristics.

Quality

We maintain the highest standards in gemstone selection, ensuring that only the finest specimens make it to our collection.

Trust

Built on decades of honest business practices, we've earned the trust of customers and partners worldwide.

Global Reach

With suppliers and customers across six continents, we bring the world's finest gemstones to your doorstep.
